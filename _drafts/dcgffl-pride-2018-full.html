Pride 2018!
We've got many many events coming up and here's a quick and easy way to make sure you know all about all of them. To get live, up to the minute information, be sure to follow each individual event on Facebook (links follow the description).

Remember that everything you do with you football family should include the hashtag #PlayProud and don't forget to spread rainbows all over Facebook.

Pride Events:

  • Final Pride Committee Meeting (Mon 6/4, 7pm)
  • Night OUT at the Nationals Stadium (Tue 6/5, 7pm)
  • Precious Opening at Pitcher's (Wed 6/6, 7pm)
  • Walk in the Capital Pride Parade (Sat 6/9, 4:30pm)
  • Team DC Sports & Cultural Village (Sun 6/10, 11am-7pm)
  • Capital Stage Beer Garden Bartending (Sun 6/10, 7pm-8:30pm)

Final Pride Committee Meeting (Mon 6/4, 7pm)

Join the fabulous members of the DCGFFL as they put the final touches on events/plans for Capital Pride Week:

  • Add your voice to our ideas
  • Help us with final preparations
  • Learn our parade dance from our fantastic choreographer Tony Smith!
  • The meeting will be held at 1618 15th St. NW

This event doesn't have a Facebook link. Email the board at dcgayflagfootball@gmail.com for more information or contact Randy Snight for information on all things Pride.

 

Team DC Presents: Night OUT at Nationals Park, June 5 (Tues, 7pm)

Come celebrate the largest LGBT Community Night in professional sports! We expect to have over 3,500 people as part of the Night OUT section at Nationals Park!

Proceeds from ticket sales contribute to Team DC’s Student-Athlete Scholarships. Scholarships are awarded each year to local college-bound LGBT student-athletes who, through their role in academics and sports, have enhanced the perception of the LGBT community.

Buy your tickets here today. Get there early!!! Gates will open at 4pm with Happy Hour fun until game time. Pre-game ceremonies start at 6:30 PM with game start at 7:10PM.

Groups of 10 or more contact Team DC directly for seats together. If you previously sent an email to us about tickets we did NOT meet the minimum requirements for a large discount and you must purchase tickets at the above link.

Precious Opening @ Pitchers (Wed 6/6, 7pm)

Oh you wanted an exciting start to your Pride weekend? Well the DCGFFL has got you covered. We are the exclusive "first occupants" of DC's hottest new gay bar, Pitchers (2317 18th St. NW). In addition to food and drink specials, you'll be able to pick up your pride tanks (which you can still order here) and learn the choreography for our fantastic Parade presence. RSVP on Facebook here.

 

 

Walk in the Capital Pride Parade (Sat 6/9, 4:30pm)

March with your team and League-mates as the DCGFFL enters into the parade for its first year as a solo contingent. Warm up your arm: We'll be tossing beads, footballs, and other swag into the crowd. Our very own Tony Smith has created a dance to #ThisIsMe which we will "perform" along the route (It’s mostly stomping and a lot of butt stuff).

RSVP on Facebook here. There you'll find more information about where we will rally (near P and 22nd). Wear your finest DCGFFL attire, or no attire at all, and get ready to strut your stuff shoulder-to-shoulder with tens of thousands. If needed, bags can be left at 1618 15th St. NW to pick up after the parade concludes.

 

 

Table at the Capital Pride Festival (Sun 6/10, 11am-7pm)

Connect with other Team DC athletes and help recruit some new ones in the first-ever Sports & Cultural Village at the Capital Pride Festival.

From 11:00am through 7:00pm, volunteer league ambassadors will talk with festival-goers about how great the league is and how they can get involved. Working the tent will earn you incentives at the festival and additional DCGFFL swag. Our booth location is TBD but should be approximately near John Marshall park. Next to the Newseum and Canadian Embassy. Come play with us!

Throughout the day, volunteer league members will participate in Team DC Sports Village activities, including a Swimsuit Competition (Time TBD), Dunk Tank (2:00-2:30), and Route Running Drill, and Flag Pulling Drill (4:30-5:30).

To volunteer, complete this form. For more information about events, RSVP on Facebook here.

 

Bartend at the Capital Stage Beer Garden (Sun 6/10, 7pm-8:30pm)

During the prime time of the Capital Pride Concert, 15 volunteer league members will bartend the Capitol Stage Beer Garden. Get great views of the performers, some free beverages, and help us contribute our proceeds and tips to the Team DC Scholarship fund!  Sign-up by filling out this form.
