The DCGFFL welcomes interested individuals to fill one (1) Open Board position for the Travel Management Board (TMB) to replace a recently vacant unaffiliated position. As an unaffiliated TMB member you look out for the greater good of the entire DC Travel program, without any bias for or against any captain or team. You make key decisions, including captain selection and player movement, with the assistance and advice of the affiliated TMB members (travel player reps).
