#### Season XXIV - Open Division Champions

Congratulations to [Pinky Swear](https://www.dcgffl.org/teams/s29-open-neon-pink) on beating [Juan Burgundy](https://www.dcgffl.org/teams/s29-open-cardinal) and winning the Open Division championship trophy!

![](/img/54945401663-4bb77b03d3-c.jpg){: width="799" height="533" loading="lazy"}

Congrats to the [Cardinal Sinners](https://www.dcgffl.org/womensplus/teams/s06-women-s-cardinal/) for taking home the Women’s+ Division Championship trophy!
